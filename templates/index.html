ABOUT OUR PROJECT WEIBO

My Weibo is a kind of Twitter website launched by the student team of software college of Beijing jiaotong university, which provides micro-blog service. Users can use weibo via web pages and E-mail.

Here you can share your micro-blog story, share your life, discover new things.
